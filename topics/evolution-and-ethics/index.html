Topic 3: Evolution and Ethics

Reading

  1. Wilson, E. O. 1975. Sociobiology: The New Synthesis. short excerpt distributed in class.
  2. Kitcher, Philip. 1993. “Four Ways of ‘Biologicizing’ Ethics.” In In Mendel’s mirror: philosophical reflections on biology. Oxford University Press, 2003. Find the eBook through Axinn Library here.

Reading questions

  • What does Wilson think the relationship between philosophical ethics and evolutionary biology should be?
  • What reasons does he offer in favor of that relationship?
  • What four kinds of relationships between evolutionary biology and philosophical ethics does Kitcher think might be embraced by someone like Wilson?
  • What arguments does he offer for, or against each of them?

See also

  • FitzPatrick, William. 2020. “Morality and Evolutionary Biology.” In The Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy.
